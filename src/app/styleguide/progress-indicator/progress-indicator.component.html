Displays a list of items to navigate in a list of components

Define a type (pages or steps) and a totalItems (number)

the component has a @Output('onSelectItem') which emit the item number when you click a page or step.

Example:

<ui-progress-indicator type="pages" [totalItems]="8"></ui-progress-indicator>
<ui-progress-indicator type="pages" [totalItems]="8" theme="purple" ></ui-progress-indicator>
 <ui-progress-indicator type="steps" [totalItems]="8" label="Label Test"></ui-progress-indicator>
