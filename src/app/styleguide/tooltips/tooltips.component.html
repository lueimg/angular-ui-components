A tooltip is a mechanism that provides additional information about something without taking up space in the view.
Use to hide non-critical information from a view. Title is optional.
Pop animation anchored to icon. Arrow aligns with icon. May display above or below the icon depending on the position within the view.

Example

Hey, I have a simple tooltip!

Tooltip with title

My tooltip has a title.
